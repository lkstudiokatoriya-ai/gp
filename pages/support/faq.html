Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Polytechnic Hub.

1. What is Polytechnic Hub?

Polytechnic Hub is an online learning platform for Polytechnic students.

2. Is registration free?

Yes, student registration is completely free.

3. How do I login?

Login using your registered mobile number and OTP.

4. How do I purchase a course?

Open the course page and click Buy Now.

5. Which payment methods are supported?

UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card and Net Banking.

6. Can I access courses after purchase?

Yes, purchased courses appear in My Courses.

7. Are demo classes free?

Yes, demo classes are free for everyone.

8. Can I download PDFs?

Yes, if download is enabled for the course.

9. Can I download videos?

Only courses that allow offline access can be downloaded.

10. What is My Courses?

It contains all purchased courses.

11. What is My Tests?

It shows purchased and attempted tests.

12. How do I reset my password?

Use the Forgot Password option.

13. Can I change my mobile number?

Yes, from Profile Settings after verification.

14. Is my data secure?

Yes, your account data is securely stored.

15. Can I use one account on multiple devices?

Yes, but login limits may apply.

16. Will I receive certificates?

Eligible courses provide completion certificates.

17. Where can I see notices?

Open the Notice Board page.

18. Where can I find PYQs?

Open the PYQ section.

19. Where are VVI Questions?

Open the VVI Questions page.

20. Can I ask doubts?

Yes, use the Doubt Center.

21. Can I contact support?

Yes, visit the Help Center or Contact Us page.

22. Does the app work offline?

Yes, many features work offline through PWA.

23. How do I update the app?

Refresh the website or reinstall the PWA if required.

24. How do I report a bug?

Use the Feedback page.

25. Can I request new courses?

Yes, submit your request through Feedback.

26. Is Polytechnic Hub available on Play Store?

It will be available after the official release.

27. How do I logout?

Open the sidebar and tap Logout.

28. How do I contact Polytechnic Hub?

Use the Contact Us page, WhatsApp, Email or Telegram.
